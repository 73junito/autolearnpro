# Independent Practice — Lab Assignment: Brake System Inspection & Bleeding

**Due:** Next lab session

**Task:** In small groups, perform a brake system inspection and complete a controlled bleed on a training rig or vehicle per instructor directions. Document findings and upload photos of key steps.

**Deliverables (submit to Canvas Assignment):**

- Inspection checklist (PDF or scanned form)
- 3 annotated photos (reservoir, caliper/bleeder, finished pedal test)
- Short reflection (300–500 words) describing what you observed, challenges, and next steps.

**Rubric (summary):**

- Safety & PPE (10%) — followed correct safety procedures
- Procedure accuracy (40%) — correct bleeding order and effective process
- Documentation (30%) — clear photos and checklist
- Reflection quality (20%) — thoughtful analysis and troubleshooting

# Welcome to Electrical Systems Fundamentals

## Course Orientation

Welcome — this course teaches fundamentals of vehicle electrical systems, safe
diagnostic procedures, and hands-on measurement.

## How to Navigate

- Use the left Modules navigation to progress sequentially. - Complete the **Safety &
Tools** module first. - Each module contains: Overview → Lesson → Practice → Quiz.

## Required Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

- Safety glasses - Insulated gloves (as needed) - Closed-toe shoes

## Tools & Software

- Multimeter (digital) - Basic hand tools - Shop access for lab activities

## Grading & Expectations

- Formative quizzes: unlimited attempts, mastery target 80%. - Lab evidence: photo or
short video upload required for shop assignments. - Participation: Discussions and short
reflections.

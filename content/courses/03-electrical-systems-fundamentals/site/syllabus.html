Syllabus — Electrical Systems Fundamentals ----------------------------------------

Course Overview --------------- This 6-week course covers automotive electrical theory, circuit analysis, measurement tools, component testing, and practical diagnostics.

Learning Objectives ------------------- - Explain basic electrical theory (voltage, current, resistance, power). - Read and analyze simple DC circuits and schematics. - Use multimeters and oscilloscopes to measure and diagnose faults. - Test and interpret battery, starter, alternator, and wiring system conditions. - Follow systematic diagnostic procedures and document results.

Weekly Outline ------------- 1. Introduction to automotive electricity; safety and tools. 2. Basic circuits, Ohm's law, series/parallel networks. 3. Measurement tools: multimeter, clamp meter, oscilloscope. 4. Charging systems: batteries, alternators, starters. 5. Wiring systems, connectors, grounds, BCM basics. 6. Diagnostics labs, capstone troubleshooting exercise, assessment.

Assessments ----------- - Weekly quizzes (low-stakes) - Two lab exercises with instructor review - Final capstone diagnostic project

Prerequisites ------------- - Basic mechanical knowledge and safety training.

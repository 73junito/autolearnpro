Lab Instructions — Charging System Check

Objective: Verify battery, starter, and alternator performance and document findings.

Steps: 1. Ensure safety: PPE, battery disconnect procedures. 2. Measure battery open-circuit voltage and record. 3. Perform a starter draw test and record voltage during cranking. 4. Start engine and measure alternator charging voltage at idle and 2000 RPM. 5. Perform voltage drop tests across battery cables and starter connections. 6. Document all readings and recommend corrective actions.

Submission: - Submit a PDF report with measurements, photos of meter screens, and recommended repairs.

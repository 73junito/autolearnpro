Lesson 2 — Basic Circuits and Ohm's Law

Objectives - Analyze series and parallel circuits. - Apply Ohm's law to compute voltage, current and resistance. - Interpret simple wiring diagrams and symbols.

Content - Series circuits, parallel circuits, equivalent resistance. - Voltage dividers and load effects. - Simple circuit solving examples relevant to automotive sensors and loads.

Lab Exercise - Build and measure simple series and parallel resistor circuits; calculate expected readings and compare with measured.

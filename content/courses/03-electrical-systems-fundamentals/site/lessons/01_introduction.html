Lesson 1 — Introduction to Automotive Electrical Systems

Objectives - Understand basic electrical concepts: voltage, current, resistance, power. - Learn safety procedures for working with vehicle electrical systems. - Identify common measurement tools and their proper use.

Content - Definitions: voltage (V), current (A), resistance (Ω), power (W). - Ohm's law and simple calculations. - Electrical hazards and safe handling of batteries and high-voltage circuits. - Overview of common tools: multimeter, clamp meter, test lamp, oscilloscope.

Lab Exercise - Demo: safe battery disconnect and basic multimeter checks (voltage, continuity).

Resources - Manufacturer service manuals - ASE study guides

Lesson 3 — Measurement Tools and Diagnostics

Objectives - Use a digital multimeter for voltage, current, resistance and continuity checks. - Understand oscilloscope basics for waveform capture and sensor diagnostics. - Apply clamp meters and in-line current measurements safely.

Content - DMM usage patterns and best practices. - Interpreting oscilloscope traces for common sensors (crank, cam, throttle position). - Safe techniques for current measurements in automotive circuits.

Lab Exercise - Capture a charging system waveform and identify ripple and regulator performance.

Resources - Oscilloscope quick-start guide - Multimeter calibration checklist

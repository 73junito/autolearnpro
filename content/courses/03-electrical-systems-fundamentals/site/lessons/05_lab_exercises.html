Lesson 5 — Labs and Capstone Troubleshooting

Objectives - Apply diagnostic techniques in multi-fault scenarios. - Record findings, perform corrective actions, and validate repairs.

Content - Guided lab scenarios: intermittent ground, parasitic draw, sensor mismatch. - Capstone: full-system electrical diagnostic on a simulated vehicle harness.

Lab Exercise - Capstone troubleshooting: students are given symptoms and must produce a step-by-step diagnosis, repair plan, and verification data.

Assessment Criteria - Accuracy of measurements, quality of documentation, correctness of fix, and validation tests.

Advanced Engine Diagnostics

Intermediate • 8 lessons

Overview

This Advanced Engine Diagnostics course equips automotive technicians with specialized skills for modern diesel engines. Focusing on complex systems like common rail injection, turbocharging, and emission controls (EGR, DPF), learners master advanced fault diagnosis using industry-standard scan tools, oscilloscopes, and pressure gauges. Through intensive hands-on labs in real-world diesel vehicle environmentsΓÇöincluding heavy-duty trucks and commercial vansΓÇöstudents practice troubleshooting sensor failures, ECU communication issues, and intricate engine control strategies. The curriculum emphasizes interpreting complex data streams and simulating real-world scenarios to pinpoint root causes efficiently. Upon completion, technicians will confidently diagnose and repair sophisticated diesel engine faults, significantly enhancing their capability to maintain high-performance, emissions-compliant diesel fleets in professional workshops.

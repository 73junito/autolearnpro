Introduction to Diesel Engines

Beginner • 6 lessons

Overview

This intensive course equips learners with essential diesel engine fundamentals for automotive technicians. Through focused practical skills development, students master core diagnostics, maintenance procedures, and troubleshooting techniques specific to diesel systems. Extensive hands-on labs provide critical experience using diagnostic tools, performing injector servicing, inspecting turbochargers, and conducting compression tests on actual diesel engines. Learners gain confidence working with commercial vehicle components like fuel injection pumps and exhaust systems. By the end, participants will effectively identify and resolve common diesel engine faults, ensuring safe and efficient operation in fleet and heavy-duty applications. This course delivers immediate, job-ready competency for diesel mechanics entering the automotive industry.

Modules

  1. Engine Fundamentals
  2. Fuel Systems
  3. Ignition & Compression
  4. Cooling & Lubrication
  5. Diagnostics & Tools
  6. Maintenance Best Practices

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand core diesel engine principles and components.
  • Perform basic diagnostics and routine maintenance tasks.
  • Apply workshop safety and best-practice procedures.
